This is used to track my progess on the 91896 and 91897 NCEA projects. 
The purpose of my outcome is to make an easy to understand and access counselling page for the school where it is easier to book with councillors and also understand and support student’s mental health. 
My target audience is students at EGGS who use the counselling page to have it be more accessible and easier to use and contact the councillors. The needs my outcome meets for them is a better way to 
access the help they might need. This outcome is worth building as I believe that there are a lot of people who might be struggling mentally but don’t know they are or are struggling mentally but don't 
know where to get help and my outcome would help them with that.
